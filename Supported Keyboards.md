# Keyboards Supported by Universal RGB

The output side of things is handled by [Soup](https://github.com/calamity-inc/Soup), which currently supports:

- SDKs
  - Razer Chroma
  - Corsair iCUE
- Devices
  - Wooting One
  - Wooting Two
  - Wooting Lekker
  - Wooting Two HE
  - Wooting Two HE ARM
  - Wooting 60HE
  - Wooting 60HE ARM
